Rules & Regulations

* Hackathon will be 13 hours long.

* Participants can form groups of 3 to 5 people max.

* Single entries are not allowed.

* Participants in a team can be from different colleges.

* Spot Registrations will be charged 200INR per person.

* Teams are not allowed to leave the college premises till 13 hours of coding followed by the judging and pitching session.

* Participants are expected to bring their own laptops, charging devices,extensions or other components that they want to use in the hackathon.

* No use of precompiled code is allowed.

* If a readymade code is seen, the team will be disqualified.

* Use of open-source repositories and APIs are allowed.

* Participants are expected to behave professionally and maintain the code of conduct.
